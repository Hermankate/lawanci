The mission for Legacy for African Women and Children Initiative (LAWANCI) is to empower, encourage and promote the safety of children and women through education, economic, health and psychological support services across the continent.
The vision Legacy for African Women and Children Initiative (LAWANCI) is to create an African society that recognizes the legacy of women and children.
Legacy for African Women and Children Initiative (LAWANCI) is a national Non-Governmental Organization incorporated on the 13th February 2018 in the Republic of South Sudan with the main aim of empowering, encouraging and promoting the safety of women and children through education, economic, health and psychological support services across the continent.

Our Objectives

What We Aim to Achieve

Leadership Development

Partnering with different stakeholders to train and mentor women and children in leadership, patriotism and National service.

Advocacy

Conduct research on issues pertinent to women and children development and disseminating findings to relevant agencies and development partners.

Economic Empowerment

LAWANCI will engage in building knowledge based on economic empowerment and development as a goal towards poverty reduction. This may be in rural development, trade, commerce and private sector development,financial management among others.

Gender mainstreaming in development

Mainstream gender perspective in development by assessing the implications for women and children of any planned action, including legislation, policies or programmes, in any area at all levels

Job skills

Training and mentoring women specially the rural women to impart upon them valuable life and job-hunting skills.

Child protection

Create and promote psychosocial therapy opportunities for vulnerable children, creating healthy women and children

Entrepreneurial Skills

Equip women and children with skills and attitudes required for successful venture planning and management, with linkages to financial services

Vocational Training

Establish a flagship national vocational training Center to equip women and children with competences and skills that are current and market based.

Peace Building

Partner with secondary schools, universities, and local communities to promote dialogue and peace-education among women and children

Research, Policy Analysis and Development

Contact periodic research in various thematic areas to respond to the policy and development needs of its entities

Raising funds

To raise, mobilize and disburse funds and other resources for empowering the women group and children organization
